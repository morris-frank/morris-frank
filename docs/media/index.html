I didn't do anything so far therefore there is nothing to report on. Notably exception in medial attention is me being cited by the as not being able to cook black salsify.

This is not true anymore so here is a working way to cook black salsify:

  1. Completely peel the salsify. Now it is not black anymore.
  2. During peeling submerge the peeled parts in a mix of water and lemon juice to keep them fresh.
  3. Cut the peeled parts up and fry them in olive oil for some minutes. Season.
  4. Pour them off with more lemon juice and some wine and let it cook shortly.
  5. Transfer roots from pan to baking dish and bake them for 45min.
  6. Eat.
