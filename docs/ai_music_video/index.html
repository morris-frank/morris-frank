Making music videos with AI

Iteratively sampling from a generative image model to generate videos conditioned on the lyrics of songs.

Let’s start right away with some results from the setup.

Lucy in the Sky with Diamonds

As all the videos are pretty intense and inconsistent, I think psychdelic rock lends itself quite naturally to this kind of video… What better than the classic Lucy in the sky from The Beatle’s Sgt. Pepper album.
